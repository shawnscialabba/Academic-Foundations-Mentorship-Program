# Academic-Foundations-Mentorship-Program

### Summary

Leveraged advanced physics and engineering expertise as an Undergraduate Teaching Assistant (UTA) to mentor over 200 students across five core engineering fundamentals.

### Program Development & Impact

*   **Mentorship:** Through strategic instructional design and targeted knowledge transfer sessions, achieved a **100% course pass rate**.
*   **Efficiency:** Delivered an estimated **$120K+ in educational value** and saved students **2,000+ collective study hours annually**.
*   **Culture:** Fostered a supportive learning environment grounded in scientific integrity and procedural discipline, which contributed to **zero academic integrity violations** and reinforced the zero-defect mindset essential for high-quality technical work.
